    Our project aims to spread knowledge about Islam in China

    To Preserve

    We preserve these manuscripts to ensure that the culture and identity of the Muslim community are not lost in the face of oppression.

    To Investigate

    We intend to track down many of the manuscripts that are considered important parts of Sino-Islamic culture.

    To Understand

    We add these manuscripts to our online archive and analyze their significance to the community.

    To Share

    By preserving these manuscripts, members of the Sino-Muslim communities and scholars of various fields are able to study these manuscripts.
